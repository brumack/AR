My name is Alexander Thomas. I am a Photographer and Director in Los Angeles, California focusing on Fashion and Music Video Production.

Originally from Philadelphia, I have traveled through a large portion of the United States pursuing my passion; creating provocative images that showcase rarely discussed facets of the human mind, and alternative lifestyles.

Fascinated with both postmodernist ideology and the human psyche, most of my work attempts to evoke a visceral feeling, while attempting to push the boundaries of societies self-imposed limits.
